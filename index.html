Features

Built different.
Works everywhere.

Nova isn't just another messaging app. It's engineered to work in conditions others can't.

💬
Real-time Chat
Instant messaging powered by Firebase Realtime Database. Messages sync across all devices in milliseconds.
📶
LAN Messaging
On the same WiFi network? Nova sends messages directly device-to-device without touching the internet.
💾
Offline Mode
No internet, no WiFi? No problem. Messages are saved locally and automatically synced when you're back online.
📸
24hr Status
Share photo updates that disappear after 24 hours. Just like you know where — but built from scratch.
🔐
Phone Auth
Login with your phone number via OTP. No passwords, no email — just your number.
🚀
Always Improving
Voice calls, group chats, and more coming soon. Nova is actively developed by Sreeman Siddi.

Smart Connectivity

3 ways to send.
1 tap to do it.

Nova automatically detects your connection and picks the best way to deliver your message.

☁️
Internet
When you have mobile data or WiFi, messages go via Firebase — fast, reliable, synced everywhere.
📶
Local WiFi
Same network as your friend? Nova sends directly device-to-device via LAN — instant and private.
💾
Offline
Completely offline? Messages are saved locally with a ⏳ indicator and synced automatically when back online.
